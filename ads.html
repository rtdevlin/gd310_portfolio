For the advertisements assignment, we were to create three advertisements, a copy-driven ad, an image-driven ad, and one that utilized both imagery and copy.

For the image-driven ad, I chose an image of a football player. For a final implementation, an actual player from the team would be used, to leverage their popularity. The image has an electric feel, which should impress a sense of excitement in viewers.

For the copy driven ad, I wanted something that could relate to both football and the mythical griffin. The line 'Be Ferocious' accomplishes this, while being succinct, increasing the odds of sticking with viewers. The font was chosen because its sharp style meshed well with the 'ferocious' message.

Finally, for the synergy ad, I chose to create an advertisement for a single game. This ad utilizes both the Griffins along with other NFL teams for added interest. The call to action is added by the background of a full stadium, which delivers a subtle message to entice customers to grab a ticket as soon as possible

All advertisements used the color scheme and branding elements of the team to reinforce brand identity
