For the final assignment, we created a website design, along with a social media layout, and another digital offering. For my digital offering, I chose a mobile application.

For the web page design, I wanted to utilize the color scheme foremost. Gold being a tertiary and lighter color worked well as a background. The faded logo was added to increase branding on the site, while also making the background more dynamic. Lastly, the layout provides news and updated content front and center, which gives users a reason to return. Each visit increases chances to purchase tickets.

For a social media website, I chose a Twitter page. Twitter provides a great way for the Griffins to provide their fans with news updates, behind the scenes content, and game updates. Twitter also allows fans to interact with the team, providing valuable feedback that the Griffins can leverage to enhance products and experiences.

Lastly, the mobile application was designed to be simple. This allows more devices to be able to run it properly, while improving user experience with less clutter. The color scheme and helmet logo are utilized for branding.
