Collateral items are items that are ancillary to the main product or service being offered. For this assignment, we were to design three collateral items for our entity. I chose a game ticket, tee shirt, and schedule

Although tickets are the main item purchased by customers, the game itself is the main draw. However, many fans still take pride in receiving their tickets or saving tickets as mementos from past games. Having a well designed, aesthetically pleasing ticket will increase the odds customers share images of their tickets.

A big draw for any sports team is apparel. Fans identify with their teams, and apparel gives them a way to display that identity while also advertising the brand. The tee shirt design incorporates the two main colors and the helmet logo. This particular design also leverages the alternate font.

Finally, schedules are a nice way for the team to give fans an easy reference to when games are played. This increases the chances games are viewed, either in person or on television. These schedules can be used on other marketing devices as well, providing fans additional utility while getting brand imaging in public places such as supermarkets and sports bars.
